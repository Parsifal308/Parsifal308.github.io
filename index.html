About me.

I am a passionate video game developer with two years of experience in the industry. During my career, I have had the opportunity to work on a variety of exciting projects.

My passion and experience are focused on designing engaging gameplay that not only entertains players, but also serves as an effective means to achieve game objectives. I believe in the importance of maintaining harmony between the game mechanics and its narrative, thus avoiding ludonarrative dissonance that can affect the player's immersion.

Outside of work, I enjoy spending time outdoors, engaging in sports and gaming. I also dedicate time to crafting stories that fuel my creativity.

Skills

I have extensive programming experience in languages such as C#, C++, Java, and Python, primarily within Unity Engine and Unreal Engine environments. Additionally, I am proficient in using essential development tools such as Git for version control, Postman for API testing, and communication platforms like Discord and Slack for effective team collaboration.

Blender, ZBrush, Substance Painter, and GIMP are my go-to tools for 3D asset creation. With these, I model, texture, and edit assets, delivering high-quality results.

I have experience coordinating small teams and tasks, ensuring projects are completed efficiently. Through clear communication and organization, I facilitate teamwork, delegate effectively, and maximize productivity.

Experience
